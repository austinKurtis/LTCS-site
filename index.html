Our specialty is crisis Medicaid planning.

Crisis Medicaid Planning:

This occurs when an individual is already in a nursing facility and needs to qualify for Medicaid immediately or will be entering a nursing home within a brief period of time.


Filing Captains:

We are a filing company working to give you complete information, satisfaction and protection. Our team of professionals are dedicated to helping those with loved ones in a nursing home or going into a nursing home get qualified for Florida Medicaid. The results speak for themselves, our ability to obtain fast qualifications, quality nursing home placement and asset protection are unmatched.


Asset Preservation:

There are many various strategies to preserve assets and qualify for the Florida Medicaid Program. Our experience in asset preservation means you will not have to give up everything you or your loved one has worked had for to get approval for Florida Medicaid.


Florida Medicaid Institutional Care Program (ICP nursing home benefits):

The Florida Medicaid Institutional Care Program considers three different criteria tests in order to determine an individual's eligibility. These tests are: medical necessity, income and assets.
